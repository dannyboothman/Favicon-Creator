11th June 2022
  • Updated to Chrome Extension Manifest 3

22nd Jan 2019
  • Text and background design elements are now split into two tabs to create more room.
10th Jan 2019
  • You can now change the border radius of your favicon. Slight scaling issues when downloading larger size favicons.

    2. Italic
    3. Underline (only available for sizes 16, 32 & 64)

  • You can now click outside the FontAwesome menu to close the menu.
04th Jan 2019
